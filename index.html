Frequently Asked Questions
What is Naoma AI?

Naoma is an AI-powered analytics platform for sales. It consists of a dashboard with a set of useful metrics, analytics of reps’ work, and analytics of sales communications. All these tools together help business owners and heads of sales make data-driven decisions, and find points of growth. Naoma provides all you need to efficiently increase your sales

Who can benefit from using Naoma?

If you have a sales team, Naoma can help to boost their performance. We have clients from a large selection of industries such as online education, IT services, e-commerce, consulting and legal services, etc. Common business roles that can benefit from Naoma include:

  • Business owners (CEO, Founder, Managing Director, President, Owner, Partner)
  • Finance professionals (CCO, CFO, Finance Director, Head of Finance)
  • Sales professionals (Sales Director, Head of Sales, Regional Sales Manager)
  • Marketing professionals (CMO, Marketing Director, Head of Marketing)
What is the technology behind Naoma?
Naoma is an AI-powered analytics system for sales teams. Our team have trained Naoma on more than 500,000 minutes of labeled sales calls with the support of leading sales professionals
What differentiates Naoma from other sales analytics tools?
Naoma, using AI algorithms, provides analytics of quantitative and qualitative metrics in sales. Naoma forms the basis for training and quick employee onboarding. Our system identifies competitors, objections, and questions from customers and makes recommendations on how each interaction can be improved
What is the process to get started with Naoma?

It’s easy!

  • Step 1: Start your free trial by submitting the quick access request form
  • Step 2: Integrate Naoma with your CRM system in a couple of clicks or contact us for assistance
  • Step 3: Naoma quickly — in only a few minutes — receives and processes your sales data
  • Step 4: Great! Naoma is now ready for use, and can easily be used to make data-drive decisions and grow revenue day by day
